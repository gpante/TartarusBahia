Tartarus Bahia
A Campaign



An adventure led by a battleworn dwarf, a misunderstood drow, a w!ld dragonborn, an alcoholic tiefling, and a musical aarakocra.



Join and follow along as they travel the untamed lands of Astera: a land of fearsome beasts and magnificent landscapes. All of which strike a stark contrast with the fortified constructions of its new settlers.



For the first time in generations, the beasts have become bolder and more aggressive. Could they be reclaiming their lost territory? Or is this the beginnings of greater machinations?
