Sample Text

Narrowly escaping the dreamscape labyrinth, our adventurers have discovered a subterranean passage. Lined along it's dark walls are deposits of mithril and moonstone, and at the other end lies the comforting glow of the moon itself.

Our adventurers, weary from travel and wary of the dream wolves' howl trek onward in seek of refuge. As they reach the edge of the tunnel, they see a temple constructed within the mountainside. It's architecture seeming to live in harmony with it's surrounding environment, the group also sees a community of surface drow tending to their evening. Or at least, most of the group.

Haha, yeah guys, I see it too. Wow!

Led tentatively by her curious mixture of intrigue and caution, Azura begins the approach towards these drow. Following suit, the rest of the party is reluctant to be left behind.

An equally tentative drow approaches the group, but this unease is somewhat released when she recognizes that Azura is of their own kind. Timidly, this drow asks if they are looking for a place to rest and the group agrees after Azura's approval.

Upon waking, our adventurers find the drow to be cooperative yet cautious. One of the braver drow say that their head priestess has left a few days ago to gather resources with a small hunting crew. The drow says that the head priestess has much to say to this specific group of adventurers.

In the meantime, our adventurers are shown hospitality and kindness in their individual pursuits while waiting for their priestess.
